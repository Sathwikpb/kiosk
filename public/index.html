AEROSPACE
LABORATORY

A CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE FOR AEROSPACE PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT & QUALIFICATION TESTING

For over 25 years, the laboratory has supported the aerospace and defence sectors through product development, specialized test facility realization, and qualification testing. The laboratory possesses expertise in the development of hydraulic pumps, actuators, valves, filters, and associated LRUs.

Key Capabilities

  • Indigenous development of aerospace hydraulic systems, components, and Line Replaceable Units (LRUs).
  • Excellence in the design, optimization, and development of hydraulic pumps, actuators, and associated subsystems.
  • Design and realization of special-purpose test rigs and customized qualification test facilities.
  • Qualification and acceptance testing of aerospace and industrial hydraulic components in accordance with national, international, and military standards.

Products Developed & Under Development

  • Pumps & Motors: Design optimization and development of axial piston pumps and hydraulic motors.
  • Actuators: Design development of linear actuators and geared rotary actuators.
  • Test Facilities: Design & development of test facilities for qualification testing of aerospace hydraulic components as per military standards.

Testing Services

  • Characterization: Flow vs pressure drop, crack opening pressure etc at various temperatures.
  • Filter Testing: Multi-pass and single-pass filter testing, collapse pressure testing, hot flow fatigue testing, cold start testing, pressure cycling, etc.
  • Pressure Tests: Static pressure testing & dynamic pressure cycling tests (Burst & proof pressure tests, pressure impulse test).
  • Environmental: Vibration, Shock, acceleration, CATH testing (as per MIL Standards).
  • LRU Qualification: Endurance testing, pressure impulse testing, high/low-temperature performance testing.
