Aerospace Product Development & Qualification Testing

Over 25 years supporting aerospace and defence through hydraulic systems development, special-purpose test facility realisation, and qualification testing to MIL standards.

KEY CAPABILITIES
  • Indigenous development of aerospace hydraulic systems and LRUs
  • Design & development of axial piston pumps, actuators, and subsystems
  • Special-purpose test rig and qualification facility realisation
  • Qualification & acceptance testing per national, international, and MIL standards
PRODUCTS
  • Pumps & Motors — Axial piston pump design optimisation and development
  • Actuators — Linear and geared rotary actuator development
  • Test Facilities — Qualification test rigs per military standards
TESTING SERVICES
  • Flow vs pressure drop, crack opening pressure at varying temperatures
  • Multi-pass / single-pass filter testing, cold start, pressure cycling
  • Burst & proof pressure, impulse tests
  • Vibration, shock, acceleration, CATH testing (MIL standards)
  • LRU endurance, high/low temperature performance

About

The CMTI Aerospace Laboratory, under the Ministry of Heavy Industries, Government of India, has been at the forefront of aerospace manufacturing technology for over 25 years. Located at Tumkur Road, Bengaluru, the lab specialises in the indigenous development of hydraulic systems, LRUs, and qualification test infrastructure for India's defence and space programmes.

Core Competencies
⬡
Hydraulic Systems Development
Axial piston pumps, actuators, control valves, LRUs
⬡
Test Facility Realisation
Special-purpose rigs designed, built & commissioned in-house
⬡
Qualification & Acceptance Testing
Per MIL-H-5440, MIL-H-8825, MIL-STD-704, JSSG-2009 & more
⬡
Environmental Testing
Vibration, shock, acceleration, CATH, thermal cycling
